Project Specification

Objective

Automating car damage assessment to provide instant financial estimates for insurance claims. By integrating AI detection with a SQLite database of car market values, the system eliminates manual bias.

The Claim Logic

Formula: Estimated Claim = Market Value × Damage Coefficient

  • Minor Damage: 0.2
  • Moderate Damage: 0.4
  • Severe Damage: 0.9

Features

  • Dynamic Car Selection (Tata, Toyota, Honda, Hyundai, VW)
  • Image processing via CNN/YOLO models
  • Secure Django User Authentication
  • History tracking for previous car analyses
